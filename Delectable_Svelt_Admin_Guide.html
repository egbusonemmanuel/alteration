DELECTABLE & SVELT: Master Tailor Dashboard

Administrator Operating Manual

Welcome to the Delectable & Svelt digital atelier. This guide will walk you through managing your avant-garde collections, viewing customer alterations, and operating your zero-gravity platform.

1. Accessing the Master Tailor Dashboard

The Admin Dashboard is strictly protected. Only authorized personnel can access it.

  1. Go to your website's homepage.
  2. Click the SIGN IN button in the top right corner.
  3. Sign in using your authorized Master Tailor email address.
  4. Once signed in, a new ADMIN icon (person silhouette) will appear in the top navigation bar. Click it.
  5. Double Authentication: You will be prompted to enter the Master Password to unlock the vault.
Your developer can provide the current Master Password (e.g., svelt2026).

Upon entering the correct password, you will enter the Dashboard.

2. Managing the Gallery (Shop)

The Gallery displays your available designs to the world.

Viewing Inventory

In the Product Inventory section of the dashboard, you will see a live table of all currently published designs, including their Name, Category, and Prices in both NGN and USD.

Posting a New Design

To add a new creation to the store:

  1. Locate the "Post New Design" form on the left side of the dashboard.
  2. Fill in the required fields:
    • Garment Name: e.g., "Obsidian Silk Blazer"
    • Category: "Bespoke", "Ready-to-Wear", or "Masterpiece"
    • Prices: Enter the price in NGN and USD.
    • Image Tag: e.g., "WEIGHTLESS"
    • Image URL: Supply a direct image link.
  3. Click PUBLISH TO GALLERY. The design will instantly appear on the live website.

3. Managing Alterations & Custom Resizing

When a user uploads a garment photo and submits their measurements through the Virtual Closet, it is sent directly to your Alterations Portal.

  1. Scroll down to the Live Alteration Bookings section.
  2. You will see a list of newly submitted garments.
  3. Review Details: You can view the customer's selected service (e.g., Fabric Reconstruction), their custom notes, and the base measurements that the Svelt AI auto-extracted from their photo.
  4. Update Status: As you work on the garment, click the animated status button under the "Actions" column to move the garment through the pipeline:
    • PENDING → STITCHING → READY FOR FITTING → COMPLETED

Support

The Delectable & Svelt portal runs on a fully real-time Supabase architectural backend. Changes made in the Admin Dashboard reflect on the live frontend instantly without refreshing.

For further technical support or updating the Master Password, please contact your lead developer.
